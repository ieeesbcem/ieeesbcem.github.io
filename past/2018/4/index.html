IEEE FIRST YEAR ORIENTATION BY CS CHAPTER

  • 04-09-2018

An orientation on IEEE CS Chapter was given to the students of Semester 1 on 04th September 2018. The session lasted for about 3 hours. The session was inaugurated by Mr. Manoj R, Student branch Counsellor. He provided an introduction to the students about IEEE CS Chapter and its activities. Students participated enthusiastically in the session. Mr. Biju K, Student branch adviser gave a brief idea about IEEE CS Chapter and and its wide opportunities. Next, a formal session on the history of IEEE CS Chapter, IEEE CS Chapter benefits and the experience on IEEE CS Chapter was taken by Mr. Anand Rajan (Chair, IEEE CS Chapter CEM) for about an hour. Namitha, Secretary IEEE CS Chapter CEM, quashed the session with her experience.

INTERSNSHIP TRAINING FOR CEM STUDENTS

  • 09-10-2018

Moving into a new world is always fascinating. You get to change your views in different ways to enjoy the sphere you're in. The purpose of the internship was to provide an opportunity for individuals to substantively perform in sectors like Web Development, Content Writing, Communication Skills and Poster design. The intern was given specific tasks and responsibilities and they was challenged to develop their capabilities and gain experience. Around Fifty IEEE Members attended the internship program.

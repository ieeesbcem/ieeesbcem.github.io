MAKE IT HAPPEN – WORKSHOP

  • 30/03/2019

IEEE IAS Chapter CEM has conducted a Two days hands on training on Design Thinking and Prototyping on 30th & 31st March, in association with OFB. OFB is an Edtech Firm providing the platform for students in schools and colleges to enrich the potential within themselves to think with an ‘out of the box' approach. It’s an innovation intervention platform dedicated to young aspiring minds and an organization with clear cut vision and runs under certain canons such as social commitments, educational assistance and analysing economic value of society. The program was jointly conducted in association with the IEEE IAS Student Branch and IEDC of College of Engineering Munnar.
